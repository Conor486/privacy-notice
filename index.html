3. How Scalis uses AI

Our AI assists recruiters and candidates by analyzing text and structured data. AI supports—but does not replace—human decision-making. We do not use facial recognition, emotion detection, or biometric categorization.

ModulePurposeTechnologyHuman oversight
Résumé analysis & matchingIdentify skills/experience that match job requirementsNatural Language Processing (NLP), semantic vector embeddingsRecruiters review shortlists before outreach

Meaningful information about the logic involved: Our models evaluate patterns aligned to the role (skills, tenure, recency, similarity to requirements) to produce a recommendation for recruiter review. Final hiring decisions are made by humans.

We conduct periodic fairness and bias testing and monitor performance to reduce disparate impact across demographic groups. A summary of our independent Algorithmic Impact Assessment (AIIA) and Bias Audit will be published in our Trust Center when available. To request access under NDA, email privacy@scalis.ai.
